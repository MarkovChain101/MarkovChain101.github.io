Bio

Ilya Yurchenko was born in Moscow, Russia and raised on Long Island, New York. Growing up in a family of academics, Ilya has always been oriented towards science, which has culminated in his current interests. Ilya is a Computer Science and Physics major at Tufts University, minoring in Philosophy and Music. In his free time, Ilya enjoys playing soccer, badminton, hockey and table tennis, listening to and creating music, and drinking good coffee.
